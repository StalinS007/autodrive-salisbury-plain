Our Detailing Services

Every Detail Covered

01 · Paint Correction

Remove swirl marks, scratches and oxidation to restore deep gloss and clarity to your paintwork.

02 · Ceramic Coating

Long-lasting, hydrophobic protection against UV, chemicals and dirt — keeping your car cleaner for longer.

03 · Interior Detailing

Deep clean and condition of all surfaces. We treat leather, shampoo upholstery and sanitise every corner.

04 · Pre-Sale Detailing

Maximise resale value with a comprehensive prep package that makes your car look its absolute best for buyers.

05 · Headlight Restoration

Restore cloudy, yellowed headlights to original clarity for better night visibility and a fresher look.

06 · Engine Bay Detailing

Thorough clean and degrease, plus dressing of plastics and hoses — and easier leak spotting.

07 · Window Tinting

Professional tint installation for privacy, UV protection and heat reduction. Various shades available.

08 · Cosmetic Touch-Ups

Stone-chip and small scratch repairs plus trim restoration for those little imperfections.

09 · Premium Hand Wash

Gentle pH-neutral hand wash with microfibre mitts to prevent swirls. Includes wheels and tyre dressing.
