Watch For These

Signs You Need Brake & Suspension Service

Spotting problems early saves you money. Book an inspection if you notice any of these.

01

Squealing or Grinding

Squeaking when braking means worn pads; grinding means metal-on-metal — act fast.

02

Vibration or Pulling

A shaky steering wheel or car pulling to one side under braking needs inspection.

03

Bouncy or Clunky Ride

Excess bouncing, nose-diving or clunks over bumps point to worn suspension.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my brake pads need replacing?

Common signs are squealing, longer stopping distances, a soft pedal or a dashboard brake warning. We'll measure pad thickness during a free brake check.

Do you do wheel alignments?

Yes. We offer precision wheel alignments to improve handling and stop uneven tyre wear, often done alongside suspension work.

Can you fix a knocking noise over bumps?

Absolutely. Knocks and clunks usually come from worn shocks, struts, bushes or control arms — we'll pinpoint and fix the cause.
