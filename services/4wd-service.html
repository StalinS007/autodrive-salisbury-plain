Signs You Need 4WD Service

01

Big Trip Coming Up

Heading outback or towing? A pre-trip 4WD inspection prevents breakdowns.

02

Clunks From Driveline

03

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you fit lift kits and aftermarket suspension?

Yes. We supply and fit suspension lift kits and heavy-duty components, set up correctly for your vehicle and how you use it.

Can you service my 4WD before a long trip?

Definitely. A pre-trip inspection covers fluids, driveline, brakes, suspension and tyres so you can head off with confidence.

Do you service the diffs and transfer case?

Yes — differential and transfer-case servicing is essential for 4WDs, especially after water crossings or heavy use.
