Signs You Need Diesel & DPF Service

Spotting problems early saves you money. Book an inspection if you notice any of these.

01

DPF Warning Light

A DPF or exhaust light means the filter is clogging and needs attention.

02

Loss of Power

Sluggish acceleration or limp mode often points to DPF or injector problems.

03

Excess Smoke or Economy Drop

Black smoke or rising fuel use signals injectors or filters need servicing.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you clean a blocked DPF instead of replacing it?

In most cases yes. A professional DPF clean is far cheaper than a new filter. We'll assess whether cleaning will restore it or if replacement is genuinely needed.

Why does my diesel keep going into limp mode?

Limp mode protects the engine, often triggered by a clogged DPF, faulty sensor or injector issue. A diagnostic scan will identify the cause.

Do you service 4WD and ute diesels?

Yes. We regularly service diesel 4WDs, utes and light commercials, including towing-heavy vehicles.
